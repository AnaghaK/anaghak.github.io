  • I believe, War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy is one of the greatest literary works of all time. The book is full of powerful scenes which Tolstoy delivers with stark reality. It ends with two epilogues, one is on the fictional story in the book and the other is basically Tolstoy's philosophical musings on history, wars and life in general. It makes for an interesting read. So if you haven't already, do have a go at the second epilogue. Here's a link, and if you rather prefer listening to it - an audio link.
