Welcome! I am a PhD candidate majoring in Computer Science at Arizona State University since Fall 2015. I am a member of Yochan research group directed by Dr. Subbarao Kambhampati. Broadly, my interests lie in the field of Automated Planning, Human-Aware AI, Human-Aware Planning.

Before joining ASU, I did my Master's at University of Southern California with a major in Computer Science. At USC, I worked on human-robot interaction related projects at Interaction Lab and on multi-agent path planning problems at IDM Lab. In Spring 2013, I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science from Visvesvaraya Technological University, India.

If you'd like to contact me, please drop me a mail at anaghak at asu dot edu.
